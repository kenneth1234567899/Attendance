About Pece Primary School

Pece Primary School is a vibrant and nurturing learning environment that caters to the educational needs of children from nursery to grade 6. We are committed to providing a holistic education that fosters academic excellence, social-emotional development, and personal growth in each child.

Located in the heart of Gulu City, Pece Primary School is a beacon of hope and opportunity for children in our community. We believe that every child has the potential to succeed, and we are dedicated to providing them with the tools and support they need to reach their full potential.

Academics

Our curriculum is designed to engage and challenge students, promoting a love of learning and preparing them for success in secondary school and beyond. We offer a wide range of subjects, including:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Art
  • Music
  • Physical Education

Admission

We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds and abilities. Our admissions process is designed to assess each child's individual needs and ensure that they are placed in a supportive and stimulating learning environment.
